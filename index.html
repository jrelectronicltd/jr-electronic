About JR Electronic Limited

JR Electronic Limited is a cross-border wholesale manufacturer and supplier specializing in electric herb grinders, smoking filters, soldering iron tools and custom packaging solutions.

We work directly with importers, distributors, retailers and brand owners worldwide — offering competitive factory pricing, flexible MOQs, and full OEM/ODM customization so you can grow your own brand with confidence.

  • Registered company with verifiable business details
  • Tiered wholesale pricing — lower cost at higher volume
  • Custom logo, packaging & graphics (MOQ 1,000 pcs)
  • Samples available before bulk production
  • Dedicated support on WhatsApp & email
